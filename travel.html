Travel · 旅行

Cities and distances that stayed a bit longer.

A small selection of journeys — night ferries, mountain towns, trains and windows. Less about destinations, more about the way light changes when you are far from home.

一些被记住的路：夜里的渡轮、山间的小镇、车窗里的城市。 不是关于“去到哪里”，更像是在离开之后，光线也变得不太一样的那些时刻。

New York, 2024 — Crossing the river by night ferry.

曼哈顿与布鲁克林之间的夜渡轮，城市在水面上被拉长了一点。

Sony A7 III · 35mm f/1.4 · ISO 3200

Guizhou, 2023 — A village slowly appearing from fog.

雾散得很慢，小镇一点一点显出来，像有人在远处翻页。

Sony A7 III · 55mm f/1.8 · ISO 400

Tokyo, 2024 — City lights through a train window.

车窗上的反光和外面的霓虹叠在一起，很难分清哪边才是真实。

Sony A7 III · 25mm f/2 · ISO 1600
