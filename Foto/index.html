Portfolio

Selected work across travel, portraits, street photography, events, and still life.

Travel

Fragments of cities and landscapes that linger after leaving.

旅行 · 在离开之后仍停留在记忆里的城市与风景。

NYC Night Ferry

A quiet crossing between boroughs, where skyscrapers dissolve into reflections.

城市与水面叠加的夜色，在渡轮的晃动中慢慢失焦。

Fog Village

A mountain town half-hidden in mist, half-remembered in light.

被雾气吞没一半的小镇，另一半停在光里和记忆里。

Portrait

Friends, strangers, and the subtle distance in between.

人像 · 朋友与陌生人，以及其间那些若即若离的瞬间。

Window Light

Soft afternoon light tracing the outline of a quiet conversation.

午后的窗边光线，勾勒出一场轻声对话的轮廓。

Neon Silence

Color, blur, and the way a city holds someone for one brief second.

霓虹与虚焦，把一个人短暂地安放在城市的怀里。

Street

Crosswalks, reflections, fleeting gestures in public light.

街拍 · 斑马线、倒影，以及路人不经意的身姿。

Crossing

A frame between red and green, where no one is fully still.

红与绿之间的几秒钟，没有人真正停下。

Rain Mirror

Streetlights breaking into pieces on wet asphalt.

被雨水打碎在地面上的街灯，拼成一面临时的镜子。

Event

Performances, talks, and the tension between stage and audience.

活动 · 舞台与观众之间的目光、灯光和情绪流动。

Under the Spotlight

Academic rituals framed like theatre, with their own fragile intensity.

像戏剧一样被聚光灯框住的学术场景，认真而脆弱。

Last Note

Musicians suspended in the half-second before silence.

演出结束前那半秒，音符和呼吸都挂在空中。

Still Life

Objects as small anchors of memory.

静物 · 把记忆暂时安放在物件里的方式。

Morning Desk

Light on paper, coffee cooling beside unfinished thoughts.

阳光落在纸上，咖啡在未写完的句子旁边慢慢变凉。

Glass Bloom

A single flower held in water and shadow.

一朵花，被水和阴影一起托住。

About

I'm Raphael (Boyan) Peng, a photographer and visual storyteller currently based between New York and Shanghai. My work drifts between travel diaries, portraits of friends, street scenes, event documentation, and quiet still life studies.

I’m interested in how light sketches the edges of a city, and how small details — hands, reflections, leftover chairs — can hold emotions that words often miss.

我是彭博炎（Raphael），在纽约与上海之间游走的摄影师与影像创作者。 我的作品在旅行、 人像、街拍、活动记录与静物之间穿梭，试图捕捉那些 不易被语言说清的小情绪、小瞬间。

我格外在意光线如何勾勒城市的边缘，也在意细节：一只手、一道倒影、 一排被遗留下来的椅子——它们常常替我们保管情绪。
