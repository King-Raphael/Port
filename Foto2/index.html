About

I'm Raphael (Boyan) Peng, a photographer and visual storyteller based between New York and Shanghai. I move between travel diaries, portraits of friends, street scenes, event documentation, and still life studies.

This site is a small selection of work — not everything, just the images that stayed with me a bit longer.

我是彭柏琰（Raphael），在纽约与上海之间游走的摄影师与影像创作者。 这里不是全部作品，只是一些暂时被留下的画面：旅行、人像、街拍、活动与静物。
